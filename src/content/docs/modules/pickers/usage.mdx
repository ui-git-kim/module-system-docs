<Aside type="caution" title="The Pickers module was retired">
There is no standalone **Pickers** module — it was never shipped as one. Its capabilities now live in the foundation and in dedicated modules:

- **Icon selection** — the starter template's [Icon Picker](/starter-template/icon-picker/) (`@/components/icon-picker`: `IconPicker`, `IconPickerDialog`, and the headless `useIconPicker` / `IconPickerGrid` / `IconRenderer` primitives).
- **Colour selection & palettes** — the [Colour Palette](/colour-palette/) and [Style](/modules/style/) modules.

Please update any bookmarks or links accordingly.
</Aside>
